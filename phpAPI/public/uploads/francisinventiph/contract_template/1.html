CONTRACT OF LEASE

 

 

 

KNOW ALL MEN BY THESE PRESENTS:

 

 

 

This CONTRACT OF LEASE made and entered into this [current_date] in Mandaluyong City, Philippines, by and between

 

 

 

 

[lessor_name] with address at [lessor_address] hereinafter referred to as the “LESSOR”

 

 

-And-

 

 

 

 

[resident_name] hereinafter referred to as the “LESSEE.”

 

 

 

 

WITNESSETH:

 

 

 

WHEREAS the LESSOR is the absolute and registered owner or appointed special power of attorney of a residential condominium unit described as [unit_name] Henares Building, Mandaluyong City, hereinafter referred to as the “LEASED PREMISES”.

 

WHEREAS, the condominium unit includes furniture, fixtures and appliances as specified

in ANNEX “A”, which is attached and forms part of the LEASED PREMISES.

 

WHEREAS, the LESSEE desires to lease the above-described property and the LESSOR is willing to lease the same unto the LESSEE, under the terms and conditions hereinafter provided.

 

NOW THEREFORE, for and in consideration of the foregoing premises, and the mutual covenants herein contained and to be performed, the LESSOR has agreed to lease, and the LESSEE hereby accepts the same by way of Lease, the aforesaid premises, under the following Terms and Conditions:

 

1)  PERIOD OF LEASE - This lease shall be for a period of one (1) year, commencing on [start_date] and ending on midnight of [end_date] renewable thereafter, upon mutual agreement of both parties within Fifteen (15) days prior to the expiration of this contract, otherwise the lease shall automatically expire and the Lessor may lease the premises to another party. The LESSEE shall give the LESSOR a Fifteen (15) day’s notice prior to the expiration of the current lease contract of its intention to renew or extend the lease.

 

 

2)  RENTAL RATES - The parties herein agree that the monthly rental of the LEASED PREMISES shall be PHP [monthly_rate] only inclusive of Condominium Association Dues.

 

 

3)  PAYMENT OF LEASE

 

a)        Seven (7) days before the scheduled move in date of [start_date] LESSEE shall pay to the LESSOR, the sum of PHP [monthly_rate] Equivalent to one (1) month’s advance rental.

 

 

b)        Eleven post-dated checks addressed to [lessor_name] for months 2-12 to be given upon move in, dated for the first day of each month.

 

If LESSEE’s checks will not be honored at any due date, LESSEE will cover for the same and shall pay the full amount of lease on the same due date. If LESSEE fails to cover said check, this will be grounds for rescission of this contract without prejudice to other legal remedies available to LESSOR.

 

 

4)  SECURITY DEPOSIT – The LESSEE agrees to pay the LESSOR the amount of [security_deposit] representing two (2) months fixed non-interest-bearing deposit which shall be held by the LESSOR during the entire term of lease. This deposit shall answer for the following obligations at the end of this lease:

 

a.     Any unpaid bills for water, electricity, cable, and internet, and:

 

b.     Any damage/s to the premises, and/or to the furniture/equipment hereon attributable to causes other than the ordinary wear and tear and acts of God.

 

c.      Any unpaid amount under paragraph #12 of this contract.

 

Such damages and unpaid bills will be deducted from this deposit and LESSOR shall provide a summary of all deductions to the LESSEE. The LESSOR shall provide receipts and documents to show in detail how the said deposit was applied to the payment of outstanding bills and/or other obligations of the LESSEE as contained in this agreement.  The balance, if any, shall be returned to the LESSEE once it is confirmed that electric and water bill are settled already, and no damages occur in the unit.

 

In the event the said two (2) month deposit is insufficient to cover for the unpaid utility bills such as electricity, water, internet, cable or damages, the LESSEE  guarantees  to  undertake payment to the LESSOR within fifteen (15) days from written notification by LESSOR.

 

5)    UTILITY CHARGES – All expenses for electricity, water, internet, telephone, cable, and other utility charges shall be for the account of the LESSEE. Additional charges due to international direct or national long-distance calls and/or upgrade of cable monthly subscription during the existence of this Contract shall likewise be for the account of the LESSEE. The LESSEE shall apply for cable and internet under their name.

 

 

6)    CUSA - The LESSOR shall pay monthly CUSA upon the execution and the duration of this Contract.

 

 

7)  SANITATION AND REPAIRS – The LESSOR undertakes to deliver the LEASED PREMISES to the LESSEE in good, clean and tenantable condition. The LESSEE in turn, shall at all times keep and maintain the premises, fixtures, and equipment, at his expense, in as good, clean, sanitary and habitable condition, as when received from the LESSOR, taking into consideration ordinary wear and tear.

 

 

The LESSOR shall be responsible for repairs on the premises, such as on water heater, plumbing, electrical and sewerage installations caused by structural defects, by storms or acts of nature, and on the equipment found to be defective, not functioning for the purpose intended for in this lease except repair/s due to the fault or negligence of the LESSEE, members of his household, guests or relatives.

 

The LESSEE shall be responsible for minor repairs and maintenance incurred through ordinary usage amounting to not more than PHP 10,000.00 (labor and materials) per occurrence, such as but not limited to:

 

 

a)     Replacement of light bulbs and fuses;

 

 

 

b)     Breakage of loss of faucet handles, shower heads, drawer or cabinet pulls and catches, keys locks, toilet mechanisms (seat and water tank covers), electrical switches or outlets, buzzers, minor faucet leaks and damaged doorknobs, clog toilet bowls, clog sinks, broken screens and similar items that may be required in the LEASED PREMISES.

 

c)      Routine service and maintenance of air-conditioners and other equipment in the premises which may be necessary for its preservation.

 

 

d)         LESSEE shall undertake the cleaning of all the aircons at least once every six months, as proven by receipt of payment of cleaning service. Should LESSEE fail to surrender any receipt the required semi-annual cleaning, the LESSOR may provide such service and deduct the cost against the Security Deposit.

 

e)     The LESSEE shall be responsible for hiring regular grease trap cleaning services at a minimum of once every six months.

 

 

 

It is further agreed that all repairs to be undertaken by the LESSOR as stipulated in this contract shall be attended immediately and in no case shall it be later than three (3) days from the time the LESSEE serves notice in writing of such needed repairs.

 

 

Should the LESSOR fail to respond to the needed repair after this period, the LESSEE shall have the option to secure services for the said repairs. In such cases, the LESSOR agrees to make direct payments for the cost of the repairs upon notice from the LESSEE. In the event the LESSEE is constrained to make such payments, the LESSOR shall reimburse the amount to the LESSEE within five (5) days from receipt of documents evidencing such payment. Conversely, the same shall apply to repairs assignable to the LESSEE.

 

 

8)  PROHIBITIONS/CARE OF THE PREMISES -

 

 

 

a)  The LESSEE shall not affix, inscribe, or paint or cause to be affixed,

 

Inscribed or painted any advertising medium on any part whether outside or inside of the unit except upon prior written permission from the LESSOR.

 

 

b) The entries, passages, corridors and stairways, shall not be obstructed or used by the LESSEE for any other purposes other than as means of entrance to and exit from the premises.

 

 

c)  The LESSEE shall not nail or drill holes into the tiled, wall-papered or concrete walls of the leased premises to avoid any damages to wall electrical lines, water pipes, etc. resulting from such works.

 

 

e)     Pest control (ants, termites, cockroaches, rodents, fleas, etc.) shall be for the account of the LESSEE.

 

f)      The LESSEE shall not smoke inside the LEASED PREMISES or within the public areas directly outside (hallway, elevator lobby, fire escape, etc.)

 

 

9)  FIRE HAZARD AND OBNOXIOUS SUBSTANCES - The LESSEE shall not keep, deposit or store in the LEASED PREMISES any obnoxious or flammable material or substance that might constitute a fire hazard.

 

 

10)               IMPROVEMENTS - The LESSEE shall not make any major structural changes, alterations or improvements in the LEASED PREMISES, which cannot be removed without destroying or defacing the premises, without the previous written consent of the LESSOR. Should such consent be given, the same improvement shall upon termination of Contract, automatically inure to the benefit of the said premises and become property of the LESSOR, without any obligation in the latter’s part to pay for its value or cost to the LESSEE. The LESSEE shall, however, have the right to introduce furniture, carpets, drapes, paintings, appliances, movable improvements ornamental and decorative objects or fixtures, and to remove said objects at his own expense at the end of the lease.

 

 

11)               RESIDENTIAL PURPOSE - The LEASED PREMISES, shall be used for residential purposes only and shall not be converted to any other purpose.  The maximum number of residents in the LEASED PREMISES is two (2) persons residing on a full-time basis. Day/night shifting of residents (that is more than two (2) persons utilizing sleeping space on a half-day basis) is strictly prohibited.

 

 

12)               PRE-TERMINATION CLAUSE –The LESSEE is bound by the twelve (12) months term of this Contract, and it shall have no right to pre-terminate this Contract.  In the event the LESSEE is assigned to another country, resigns or otherwise terminates his employment, or is gravely ill/injured or transfers to another property, the LESSEE shall have the right to designate another person to occupy the LEASED PREMISES for the remainder of the term of this Contract with the prior written approval of the LESSOR.

 

 

In the event the LESSEE has no other designated occupant, the LESSEE shall forfeit their right to the refund of the security deposit and remaining payment up to the following post-dated check due, without prejudice to other remedies afforded to LESSOR by law.

 

 

13)               COMPLIANCE WITH RULES AND REGULATIONS - The LESSEE agrees to abide by the existing rules and regulations of the Condominium Property Management Corporation, the Project Master Deed of Restrictions, and any other laws, ordinances, rules and regulations promulgated by competent authorities affecting the occupancy of the LEASED PREMISES.

 

14)               SUB-LEASE - The LESSEE shall not subject or sublease in whole or in part, the LEASED PREMISES, nor assign this Contract of Lease to any other party other than the officers of the LESSEE, without prior written consent to the LESSOR.  Any sublease of said premises or any assignment of such Contract of Lease shall be null, and void and the LESSOR shall have the right to extra judicially terminate this contract and demand the LESSEE to immediately surrender the leased premises at once. All unused portions of the advance rentals as provided for in Item #3 shall likewise be forfeited in favor of the LESSOR.

 

 

15)               THIRD PARTY LIABILITY - The LESSEE, during the effectivity of this Contract and his occupancy of the LEASED PREMISES, shall hold the LESSOR free and harmless from any damages, liability or responsibility to any person or property arising out of or as a consequence of the use of the LEASED PROPERTY by the LESSEE, his agents employees, domestic helpers and guests.  When such damage or liability is caused by fortuitous events, or acts of God, such as typhoons, earthquakes, floods, etc. which is beyond the control of the LESSEE, the latter shall not be liable to the LESSOR.

 

 

16)                INSPECTION OF THE LEASED PREMISES – The LESSOR or his DULY authorized representative, shall have the right to inspect/enter the LEASED PREMISES at a reasonable time, with at least one

(1) day written notice, for the purpose of checking the premises for any violation of the terms and conditions of the Contract and/or for repairs and inspection which may be deemed necessary for the operation and maintenance of the premises.

 

 

17)               FORCE MAJEURE/ SUBSTANTIAL DAMAGE TO THE PREMISES – In case of damage to the LEASED PREMISES as may be occasioned by fire not attributable to the LESSEE, earthquake, typhoon, or any other natural calamities, and war or similar concerted acts of men, LESSEE shall give immediate notice thereof to the LESSOR. Upon receipt of such notice, the LESSOR shall therefore endeavor and see to it that the LEASED PREMISES are restored in tenantable conditions at the earliest time. Expenses for the repair and restoration as indicated above shall are for the account of the LESSOR.

 

 

If however, the LEASED PREMISES or any portion thereof shall be so destroyed to make it not suitable for occupancy, either party may demand for the rescission of this Contract of Lease. In such case, the security deposit mentioned in provision No. 4 (less any unpaid and/or outstanding bills due to the LESSEE) and all the unapplied portions of the advanced rentals shall be refunded by the LESSOR to the LESSEE not later than thirty (30) days without the necessity of demand.

 

 

Nothing in this provision shall exempt the LESSEE from his liability to the LESSOR for damages caused to the LEASED PREMISES due to his fault or negligence or that of his family, agents, household help, employees or guests.

 

18)               MORTGAGE OR SALE OF PROPERTY - In the event that the ownership of the LEASED PREMISES is sold, transferred or conveyed, mortgaged, or encumbered to a third party during the term of this lease or any renewal hereof, the LESSOR undertakes to inform in writing the said third party, his/her successors, executors, administrators or assigns, of existence of the lease contract prior to or at  the  time  of  the  sale,  transfer,  conveyance,  mortgage  or  encumbrance and to require the incorporation in the DEED OF ABSOLUTE SALE, OR CONVEYANCE, either by reference or otherwise of the terms and conditions of the Contract of Lease, so as to form part and parcel thereof. Moreover, the LESSOR undertakes to require said third party to respect the LESSEE’s right under this contract as well as all the terms and conditions of the lease.

 

 

In the event of the bonafide sale of the leased property, the LESSOR shall transfer and pay to the Vendee the security deposit and remainder of the advanced rentals which are yet to be paid to future rent under the terms of this Contract of Lease. The LESSOR shall see to it that the Deed of Sale provides that the Vendee shall treat and apply said security deposit and the remainder of the advanced rentals in accordance with the terms of the Contract of Lease.

 

 

19)                 RETURN OF PREMISES - Upon termination of this Contract for any reason whatsoever, the LESSEE shall immediately vacate the LEASED PREMISES and return possession thereof to the LESSOR with all the keys thereto appertaining, devoid of all occupants, furniture and articles, and other movable effects of any kind owned by the LESSEE. The LEASED PREMISES shall likewise be returned to the LESSOR by the LESSEE in as clean and good condition as when received from the LESSOR at the start of the lease, taking into consideration the ordinary wear and tear.

 

 

Fifteen (15) days prior to the return of the LEASED PREMISES, the LESSOR may show the premises to prospective tenants during reasonable hours with one (1) day prior notice to the LESSEE.

 

 

20)               VIOLATION OR BREACH OF CONTRACT –The LESSEE hereby agrees that all the provisions contained in this Contract shall be deemed as conditions and covenants and that the LESSOR shall have the right to immediately terminate and cancel this Contract. Should the LESSEE violate any/or all of said conditions, including, but not limited to the failure to pay rent or any amount due hereunder on the date specified for its payment.

 

 

In the event of any breach of the terms and conditions of this Contract, the LESSOR shall be entitled to exercise, cumulatively or separately, at its discretion, any of the following remedies:

a.     Open, enter, occupy, padlock, secure, enclose, fence and otherwise take full and complete physical possession and control of the LEASED PREMISES. For this purpose, the LESSEE hereby appoints the LESSOR, its authorized agents, employees and/or representatives as its duly

 

authorized attorneys-in-fact, with full power and authority to perform such acts;

 

b.     In case the LESSEE fails to pay or fund a postdated check due thereon, without any valid reason therefore, any monthly rental despite demand from the LESSOR, the latter shall have the right require the LESSEE to vacate the LEASED PREMISES, to discontinue and cut utility lines of the unit including power, water, telephone and other services until the LESSEE complies with his obligations.

 

c.      Collect from the LESSEE the rent and any amount due under this Contract and the penalty and interest charges of 3% per month of thereon;

 

 

d.     Forfeit the entire security deposit; in the case of damages or outstanding account for association dues, water and electricity charges, and all other charges for applied utilities during the period of tenancy, the LESSEE agrees to settle this in full.

 

 

e.     Collect any other damages, whether actual or consequential, which the LESSOR may have suffered or incurred.

 

 

21)               COMPLETE AGREEMENT. -- This Contract constitutes and embodies the entire and complete agreement between the parties and no other terms and conditions, verbal or otherwise, not herein expressly contained, shall affect, change, modify, be added to or in any manner alter the provisions herein agreed upon unless such change, modification, addition, or alteration is expressly stipulated in writing and duly executed, signed by both parties and notarized.

 

 

 

 

 

 

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto set their hands at Mandaluyong City, Metro Manila, Philippines, this [current_date]
